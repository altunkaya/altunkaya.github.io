Teaching

I was a teaching assistant for several ICS courses at UHM:

  • ICS311 Analysis of Algorithms: Assisted the professor in the preparation of algorithms assignments. Prepared and presented a few lectures. Office hours held to assist the students.
  • ICS332 Operating Systems: Assignments graded and office hours held to assist the students.
  • ICS212 Program Structure: Prepared assignments and held C/C++ lab sessions twice a week for a class of ~15 students. Office hours held to assist the students.
  • ICS141 Discrete Mathematics for Computer Science I: Assignments graded and office hours held to assist the students.
